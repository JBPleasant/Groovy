our story

Driven by a lifelong passion for music, Elijah embarked on a journey to create a haven for music lovers. Groovy School emerged, a vibrant tapestry of melodies, camaraderie, and transformative learning, where music ignited dreams and fostered a community bound by the universal language of harmony.
